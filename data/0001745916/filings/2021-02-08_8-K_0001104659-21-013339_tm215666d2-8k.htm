Item 8.01 Other Events.

 

On February 8, 2021, PennyMac Financial Services, Inc. (the “Company”) issued a press release announcing its intention to offer $500 million aggregate principal amount of senior notes due 2029 in a private offering (the “Offering”) that is exempt from the registration requirements of the Securities Act of 1933, as amended. A copy of the press release is filed as Exhibit 99.1 and incorporated herein by reference.

 

In connection with the Offering, the Company also disclosed certain additional information in related offering memorandum in advance of the filing of its Annual Report on Form 10-K for the year ended December 31, 2020, including updated Risk Factors disclosure and updated Legal Proceedings disclosure related to the Company. A copy of these updated disclosures is attached hereto as Exhibit 99.2 and is incorporated herein by reference.

 

This Current Report on Form 8-K, including Exhibits 99.1 and 99.2, does not constitute an offer to sell or the solicitation of an offer to buy any security and shall not constitute an offer, solicitation or sale of any security in any jurisdiction in which such offering, solicitation or sale would be unlawful.
