This Tender Offer Statement on Schedule TO (this “Schedule TO”) relates to the tender offer by Heisman Merger Sub, Inc., a Delaware corporation (“Purchaser”) and a wholly owned subsidiary of Mr. Cooper Group Inc., a Delaware corporation (“Mr. Cooper”), for all of the outstanding shares of common stock, par value $0.0000000072 per share (“Shares”), of Home Point Capital Inc., a Delaware corporation (“Home Point”), at a price of $2.33 per share, net to the seller in cash, without interest, and subject to any required withholding taxes, upon the terms and conditions set forth in the offer to purchase, dated May 26, 2023 (the “Offer to Purchase”), a copy of which is attached as Exhibit (a)(1)(A), and in the related letter of transmittal (the “Letter of Transmittal”), a copy of which is attached as Exhibit (a)(1)(B), which, as each may be amended or supplemented from time to time, collectively constitute the “Offer.”

All the information set forth in the Offer to Purchase, including Schedule I thereto, is incorporated by reference herein in response to Items 1 through 9 and Item 11 of this Schedule TO, and is supplemented by the information specifically provided in this Schedule TO.
