The following is an email sent from Varun Krishna, the Chief Executive Officer of Rocket Companies, Inc., to employees of Mr. Cooper Group Inc. on April 4, 2025.

 

Building the future of homeownership together

Mr. Cooper Team,

I hope you are feeling as excited as I am about Mr. Cooper and Rocket joining forces. Together we will be on a journey to build the future of homeownership.

Mr. Cooper is a force in the industry, and I have long admired the business you have built together. Early in my time as Rocket CEO I had the opportunity to meet Jay and we immediately connected on our companies’ shared culture and vision. As we explored bringing our two companies together, I had the opportunity to dive deeper into your organization, and what I have learned has only reinforced how confident I am that your team will strengthen Rocket’s culture and company overall.

Mr. Cooper’s core values of challenging convention, being champions for your customers and cheerleaders for your team are very similar to Rocket’s ISMs, which are the foundation of Rocket and explain the soul of our company. Mr. Cooper’s purpose of keeping the dream of homeownership alive fits nicely in Rocket’s mission to Help Everyone Home.

As I shared with the Rocket organization, we are creating the first-ever integrated homeownership platform in America. Over my time in the space, my “aha moment” was realizing that a homeownership platform is built from three main parts: home search, mortgage origination and servicing, and that no one has dared to connect them and create a network effect.

Redfin, our other recent acquisition announcement, will accelerate our growth in search and real estate top-of-funnel. By combining Rocket and Mr. Cooper, we will take major leaps forward in both origination and servicing, allowing us to make homeownership more accessible for consumers across all 50 states. One super funnel.

I want to also emphasize that you are a critical factor to the success of the combined company and our ability to deliver on our vision. Over the next several months, our teams will work together to develop a thoughtful integration plan that builds on our collective strengths and learnings from each other.

We are so excited to welcome the Mr. Cooper team to Rocket and we’re thankful that we will continue benefiting from Jay’s leadership as he works alongside me as President and CEO of Rocket Mortgage.

Thanks for everything you have done to make Mr. Cooper such a great company and the perfect fit to join Rocket as we accelerate our pursuit of building America’s first homeownership platform together.

Our future is bright.

-varun
