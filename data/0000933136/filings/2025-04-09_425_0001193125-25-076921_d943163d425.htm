Mr. Cooper: Direct-to-Consumer Customer Talking Points (Trigger Leads)

April 9, 2025

HANDLING TRIGGER LEADS WITH COMPETITORS

What is a “trigger lead,” and why am I being contacted by another mortgage company?

A “trigger lead” is a lead generated when your credit report is run as part of the mortgage application process. The lead indicates to other mortgage lenders that you may be in the market for a purchase, refinance or an equity loan. Rocket may be one of the companies contacting you because of that trigger notification they receive. I can help you move forward business as usual.

What will happen to my current loan service once the acquisition is complete?

Once the acquisition is complete, the combined company will service your loan under the Rocket Mortgage brand. Ahead of that change, we will be in touch with all the information you’ll need. For now, everything is staying the same. Rest assured: No terms or conditions of your mortgage will be affected by this transition, and soon you will get to enjoy all the benefits that both Mr. Cooper and Rocket Mortgage have to offer.

Can I still work with the same loan officer after the acquisition?

Yes, as your loan officer, I will continue to be available to you, and we’re committed to maintaining the same high level of service. It’s important to keep in mind that the acquisition won’t be complete until later this year, and your current [refinance/purchase/etc.] should be complete by that time.

We’ll ensure you have continued support throughout the entire process.

Should I wait until the acquisition is complete to apply for a loan or refinance?

You and I can move forward on your loan/refinancing now without worrying about the acquisition, especially knowing it isn’t expected to close until later this year.

We’ll continue to process your loan with the same care and attention.

The process won’t be disrupted, and you won’t need to start anything over when the acquisition is finalized.

How long will it take for the acquisition with Rocket and Mr. Cooper to finalize?

The acquisition is expected to be finalized toward the end of this year, between October to December. In the meantime, you can expect to continue receiving the same excellent service you’ve been getting. We’ll keep you informed every step of the way, and we’ll make sure the transition process is smooth.

Why should I stay with Mr. Cooper instead of switching to Rocket Mortgage now?

By staying with us, we can ensure that the transition will be as smooth as possible.

There won’t be any interruption in your loan being serviced, and you won’t have to worry about starting over with a new lender. We already have your information, and we’ll continue to provide you with the same level of service you’ve come to expect from us.
