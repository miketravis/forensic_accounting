Item 8.01.
Other Events.

On May 10, 2023, Mr. Cooper Group Inc., a Delaware corporation (the “Company”), issued a press release announcing the execution of an Agreement and Plan of Merger (the “Merger Agreement”), dated as of May 10, 2023, by and among the Company, Home Point Capital Inc., a Delaware corporation (“Home Point”), and Heisman Merger Sub, Inc., a Delaware corporation and wholly owned subsidiary of the Company. A copy of the press release is attached as Exhibit 99.1 to this report and incorporated herein by reference.
 
In connection with the announcement of the Merger Agreement, the Company intends to provide supplemental information regarding the proposed transaction to analysts and investors. The slides that will be made available are attached hereto as Exhibit 99.2 and are incorporated herein by reference.
