Item 1.01 Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement.

On March 25, 2022, QL Ginnie EBO, LLC, a Delaware limited liability company, as seller, together with QL Ginnie REO, LLC, a Delaware limited liability company, as REO subsidiary, and Rocket Mortgage, LLC, a Michigan limited liability company (the "Company") and indirect subsidiary of Rocket Companies, Inc., as guarantor, and JPMorgan Chase Bank, National Association, as buyer, entered into Amendment No. 3 to the Master Repurchase Agreement dated as of December 14, 2017, as amended (the "Master Repurchase Agreement") and amendment no. 4 to the related pricing side letter, which extended the termination date of the Master Repurchase Agreement from March 23, 2023 to March 13, 2024 and effectuated certain other technical changes to the Master Repurchase Agreement. The facility amount remains unchanged at $2.6 billion.

The foregoing description of the Master Repurchase Agreement, as amended, does not purport to be complete and is subject to, and qualified in its entirety by reference to the full text of the Amendment No. 3 to the Master Repurchase Agreement, a copy of which will be filed with the quarterly report on Form 10-Q of Rocket Companies, Inc. for the period ending March 31, 2022.

Following the execution of the amendments, as of March 25, 2022, the total funding capacity of the Company, including pursuant to all master repurchase agreements, early funding facilities, unsecured lines of credit, MSR lines of credit and early buy out facilities, remains unchanged at $33.8 billion. This figure compares with $34.2 billion and $29.30 billion as of December 31, 2021 and December 31, 2020, respectively.
