Item 1.01 Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement.

Citibank, N.A. Master Repurchase Agreement Renewal
On December 10, 2024, Rocket Mortgage, LLC (the "Company"), a Michigan limited liability company and indirect subsidiary of Rocket Companies, Inc., as Seller, and Citibank, N.A. ("Citibank"), as Buyer, entered into Amendment Number Six to the Master Repurchase Agreement (the "MRA Amendment") and the related Amendment Number Ten to the Pricing Side Letter for the Master Repurchase Agreement, which extended the expiration date of the existing Master Repurchase Agreement dated as of September 4, 2019 by and between Citibank, as Buyer, and the Company, as Seller (as amended, the "Citibank Master Repurchase Agreement"), from November 6, 2025 to December 10, 2026 and effectuated certain other technical changes to the Citibank Master Repurchase Agreement.

Citibank, N.A. MSR Facility Amendment
On December 10, 2024, the Company, as borrower, entered into Amendment Number Four to the MSR Facility (the "MSR Amendment") with Citibank, as lender. The MSR Amendment extended the expiration date of the existing MSR Facility from November 6, 2025 to December 10, 2026 and effectuated certain other technical changes to the MSR Facility.

The foregoing descriptions of the MRA Amendment and MSR Amendment do not purport to be complete and are subject to, and qualified in their entirety by reference to the full text of the MRA Amendment and MSR Amendment, copies of which will be filed with the annual report on Form 10-K of Rocket Companies, Inc. for the period ending December 31, 2024.

Following the execution of the MRA Amendment and the related Amendment Number Ten to Pricing Side Letter for the Citibank Master Repurchase Agreement, as of December 10, 2024, the total funding capacity of the Company, including pursuant to all master repurchase agreements, early funding facilities, unsecured lines of credit, MSR lines of credit and early buy out facilities, was $27.0 billion. This figure compares with $24.5 billion as of September 30, 2024 and $24.3 billion as of December 31, 2023.
