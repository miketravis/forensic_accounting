Item 7.01     Regulation FD.

Rocket Companies, Inc. (the "Company") made the following disclosures in relation to market events, including the recent closures of regional banks:

•The Company does not hold cash deposits or securities at Silicon Valley Bank or Signature Bank, and has no business relationship with - or direct exposure to - either bank;
•The Company’s corporate cash accounts are held with large global money center banks;
•The Company’s warehouse line providers are all with large global money center banks or their affiliates, and;
•Client escrow funds are held in fully insured deposit accounts with large global money center banks.

The information furnished pursuant to Item 7.01 shall not be deemed “filed” for purposes of Section 18 of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, as amended (the “Exchange Act”) or otherwise subject to the liabilities under that Section and shall not be deemed to be incorporated by reference into any filing of the Company under the Securities Act of 1933, as amended, or the Exchange Act.
