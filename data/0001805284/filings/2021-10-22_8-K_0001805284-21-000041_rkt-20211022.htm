Item 1.01 Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement.

On October 22, 2021, Rocket Mortgage, LLC (the "Company"), a Michigan limited liability company and indirect subsidiary of Rocket Companies, Inc., as Seller, and Credit Suisse First Boston Mortgage Capital LLC, as Administrative Agent, Credit Suisse AG, a company incorporated in Switzerland, acting through its Cayman Islands Branch, as buyer, Alpine Securitization LTD, as buyer and other Buyers from time to time party to the Third Amended and Restated Master Repurchase Agreement, entered into Amendment No. 18 to the Pricing Side Letter for the Third Amended and Restated Master Repurchase Agreement (the "Amendment"), which extended the termination date of the existing Third Amended and Restated Master Repurchase Agreement dated as of May 24, 2017, as amended, by and between Credit Suisse First Boston Mortgage Capital LLC, as Administrative Agent, Credit Suisse AG, a company incorporated in Switzerland, acting through its Cayman Islands Branch, as buyer, Alpine Securitization LTD, as buyer and other Buyers from time to time party thereto and the Company (the "Master Repurchase Agreement") from October 22, 2021 to October 20, 2023 and effectuated certain other technical changes to the Master Repurchase Agreement.

Following the execution of the Amendment, as of October 22, 2021, the total funding capacity of the Company, including pursuant to all master repurchase agreements, early funding facilities, unsecured lines of credit, MSR lines of credit and early buy out facilities, remains unchanged at $33.9 billion. This figure compares with $29.30 billion and $19.13 billion as of December 31, 2020 and December 31, 2019, respectively.
