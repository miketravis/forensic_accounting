Item 5.02     Departure of Directors or Principal Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Principal Officers.

As previously announced on June 26, 2024, Rocket Companies, Inc. (the “Company”) has appointed Heather Lovier as the Company’s Chief Operating Officer, effective June 20, 2024. This Current Report on Form 8-K/A (“Amendment No. 1”) amends Item 5.02 of the Company’s Current Report on Form 8-K filed on June 26, 2024 (the “Original Filing”) to disclose certain compensation arrangements in connection with Mrs. Lovier’s appointment, which arrangements had not yet been determined when the Original Filing was filed. This Amendment No. 1 supplements the Original Filing and should be read in conjunction with the Original Filing.

On August 26, 2024, the Compensation Committee of the Board of Directors of the Company approved a base salary for Mrs. Lovier in the amount of $600,000. Mrs. Lovier will also be eligible for a target bonus of 100% of her base salary for 2024. In addition, Mrs. Lovier received a grant of restricted stock units (“RSUs”) for 60,153 shares on August 26, 2024, which will vest in six equal, semi-annual installments over a period of three years. The RSUs will be subject to the terms and conditions of the Company’s Omnibus Incentive Plan and an award agreement substantially in the form of the publicly filed RSU Agreement. Mrs. Lovier entered into an employment agreement and indemnification agreement with the Company each substantially in the form of the publicly filed Executive Employment Agreement and Indemnification Agreement, respectively.
