Item 1.01 Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement.

On September 16, 2021, Rocket Mortgage, LLC (the "Company"), a Michigan limited liability company and indirect subsidiary of Rocket Companies, Inc., as Seller, Morgan Stanley Bank, N.A., as Buyer and Morgan Stanley Mortgage Capital Holdings LLC, as Agent for the Buyer, entered into Amendment Number Five to Master Repurchase Agreement and Amendment Number Four to Pricing Side Letter for the Master Repurchase Agreement (the "Amendments"), which extended the expiration date of the existing Master Repurchase Agreement dated as of October 17, 2019, as amended, by and between Morgan Stanley Bank, N.A., as Buyer and Morgan Stanley Mortgage Capital Holdings LLC, as Agent for the Buyer, and the Company (the "Master Repurchase Agreement") from September 16, 2021 to September 15, 2023 and effectuated certain other technical changes to the Master Repurchase Agreement.

The foregoing description of the Master Repurchase Agreement, as amended, does not purport to be complete and is subject to, and qualified in its entirety by reference to the full text of the Amendment Number Five to the Master Repurchase Agreement, a copy of which will be filed with the next quarterly report on Form 10-Q of Rocket Companies, Inc.

Following the execution of the Amendments, as of September 16, 2021, the total funding capacity of the Company, including pursuant to all master repurchase agreements, early funding facilities, unsecured lines of credit, MSR lines of credit and early buy out facilities, decreased to $33.9 billion. This figure compares with $29.40 billion and $19.13 billion as of December 31, 2020 and December 31, 2019, respectively.
