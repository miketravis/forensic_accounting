The following is an email sent from Varun Krishna, the Chief Executive Officer of Rocket Companies, Inc. (“Rocket”), to employees of Redfin Corporation (“Redfin”) on May 2, 2025.

 

Email to RedfinDistros
From: Varun Krishna
Date: Friday, May 2
Subject: Redfin Visit

 

Happy Friday Redfin team,

 

This week, a few of us from Rocket had the privilege of spending time in Seattle.

 

We had a productive and full agenda – with business reviews and product deep dives, and surfacing the key decisions we need to make together on integration.  

 

I continue to be SO impressed with the Redfin team.  At every turn, we saw a team that moves with intention and speed, knows the numbers and executes with discipline and constructive dialog. It was both refreshing and familiar – as these are all values we hold at Rocket, and yet…..we can learn so much from each other.

 

We also had the chance to talk about the future. During the all-hands meeting, I spoke to our vision for a connected homeownership experience – powered by the combined strengths of Redfin, Rocket and Mr. Cooper.

 

As we think about our future, I want to emphasize that we are bringing thoughtful, relentless focus to the integration process. 

 

Thank you to everyone who has continued to offer us a warm welcome at your office. I find myself leaving each trip to Seattle with deep appreciation for all of you and the opportunity we have ahead.

 

As I’ve said to our Rocket team, we are building a generational company together.

 

Grateful,
–varun
