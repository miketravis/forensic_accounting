The following are talking points for a video message delivered by Varun Krishna, the Chief Executive Officer of Rocket Companies, Inc., to Rocket employees on March 31, 2025.

 

 

Varun video talking points (to all Rocket team members)

 

Set the stage:

 

1.I’m coming back to you with another big announcement for Rocket. Just a few minutes ago, we announced that we have entered into an agreement to acquire Mr. Cooper, America’s largest mortgage servicer.

 

2.I know this comes just a few weeks after our news about signing a deal to acquire Redfin, so let me go deeper on our strategy and what’s ahead.

 

3.When I joined Rocket, I said we were a sleeping giant.

 

4.We have been the longstanding leader in mortgage, but our potential has always extended far beyond that.

 

5.I’ve realized that our calling is to build a homeownership platform, built with three parts: mortgage, home search and servicing.

 

6.Through the amazing work of this team, we have attracted two fantastic companies with deep legacies in the industry who want to join us at Rocket. Together, we will team up to build the future of homeownership together.

 

Proof Points:

 

7.By joining forces with Mr. Cooper – the nation’s largest mortgage servicer – we make a big move forward in servicing. Rocket is the established leader in mortgage today, and acquiring Redfin will advance our home search by bringing in the nation’s largest online brokerage.

 

8.Through this combination, we will hold a combined $2.1 trillion in mortgage servicing, growing our serviced clients from almost 3 million to almost 10 million. Imagine what’s possible when we combine that servicing book with our J.D. Power winning client service.

 

 

  

 

9.Stop and think about this.  Our servicing book will represent one in every six homeowners with a mortgage! This number is impressive but also shows how much room there is for growth.

 

10.This increase in servicing volume will allow us to help more clients while also tripling the size of our data pool, allowing us to lean in even harder in the AI space and deliver clients more personalized, relevant solutions – all while reducing costs…for us and them.

 

11.Just as importantly, the increase in servicing will give Rocket a more balanced business model with more revenue and earnings stability in all rate environments.

 

About Mr. Cooper and Foreshadow Changes:

 

12.As we think about these acquisitions, I want to double down on my commitment to stay true to Rocket culture and our ISMs.
   
 13.I know this is a lot of news to take in at once. My ask of all of you is to keep your focus – the most important thing each of us can do right now is to continue executing and living our ISMs.

 

14.For now, we need to operate completely separately from Mr. Cooper until the deal closes – which we expect to be in the fourth quarter of this year.

 

15.As we wrap up, I hope you’ll reflect a bit on this moment. We have created a company in Rocket that has attracted these two powerhouses to want to join us on our mission. I’m exceptionally proud of all of you, and the work we have done – our future is so bright.

 

16.As you take in this news, you may have questions – we have linked an FAQ where you can find more information.
   
 17.Thanks for tuning in and I hope you are as excited about our future as I am. Have a great week.
