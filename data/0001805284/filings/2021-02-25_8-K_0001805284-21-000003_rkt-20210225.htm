Item 7.01 Regulation FD Disclosure

On February 25, 2021, the Company’s Board of Directors authorized and declared a cash dividend (the “Special Dividend”) of $1.11 per share to the holders of the Company’s Class A common stock, par value $0.00001 per share (the “Class A Common Stock”). The Special Dividend will be paid on March 23, 2021 to holders of the Class A Common Stock of record as of the close of business on March 9, 2021. The Company will fund the Special Dividend from cash distributions of approximately $2.2 billion by RKT Holdings, LLC to all of its members, including the Company.
