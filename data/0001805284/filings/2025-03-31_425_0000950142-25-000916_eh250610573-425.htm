The following is an email sent from Varun Krishna, the Chief Executive Officer of Rocket Companies, Inc., to employees of Redfin Corporation, on March 31, 2025.

 

 

Varun email to all Redfin

 

Good morning Redfin team and happy Monday,

I’m reaching out to you this morning because we just announced big news at Rocket: we are entering into an agreement to acquire Mr. Cooper, the nation’s largest mortgage servicer.

 

Since joining Rocket 18 months ago, I often get asked the question “Are you a mortgage company or a technology company?” to which I respond, “Neither. We are a homeownership company.”

 

Over time, I’ve realized that a homeownership platform is built from three main parts: home search, mortgage origination and servicing. That’s why our mission is to help everyone home.

 

For me this realization sits behind our recent acquisition announcements and why we are so excited Redfin will join Rocket. I have loved using your home search and agent platform for decades and as I connected with Glenn and learned more about your business, we knew your team would be critical in accelerating us toward being a true homeownership company together.

 

With Rocket and Redfin poised to change the game in home search and loan origination, it was time to grow our scale in the servicing aspect of the homeownership platform. We view servicing and the managing of a mortgage for our clients as an opportunity to create lifelong relationships.

 

Once Mr. Cooper joins Rocket, we will service $2.1 trillion in mortgages - increasing our current servicing client base from about 3 million clients to nearly 10 million clients. That’s one in every six homeowners in America with a mortgage that we can create more benefit for via recapture to our top of funnel.

 

 

   

 

 

Our belief is that together, our three companies will build the future of homeownership. That future will be powered by AI, supercharging our amazing teams, unlocking new benefits and increasing savings for clients.

 

The other important point to underscore is culture. The soul of Rocket is held in our culture, and we found strong alignment with both Redfin and Mr. Cooper - which was a requirement for us in these processes.

 

Similar to our recent announcement together, Rocket and Mr. Cooper will continue to operate completely separately until that deal closes, which we expect to be in the fourth quarter of this year.

 

I hope you are as excited as we are to build the future of homeownership together. We believe the whole of Redfin, Rocket and Mr. Cooper together is far greater than the sum of its parts - and I can’t wait to get started building together as quickly as possible.

 

The future is bright.

 

-varun
