Item 1.01 Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement.

On July 16, 2021, Quicken Loans, LLC (the “Company”), a Michigan limited liability company and indirect subsidiary of Rocket Companies, Inc., as borrower, entered into Eighteenth Amendment (the “Amendment”) to Master Repurchase Agreement (the “Master Repurchase Agreement”) with JPMorgan Chase Bank, N.A., as a Buyer and Administrative Agent for the Buyers, and the other Buyers from time to time as a party thereto. The Amendment extends the maturity date of the Master Repurchase Agreement to April 21, 2023 and effectuates certain other technical changes to the Master Repurchase Agreement.

The foregoing description of the Amendment does not purport to be complete and is subject to, and qualified in its entirety by reference to the full text of the Amendment, a copy of which will be filed with the next quarterly report on Form 10-Q of Rocket Companies, Inc.

Following the execution of the Amendment, as of July 16, 2021, the total funding capacity of the Company, including pursuant to all master repurchase agreements, early funding facilities, unsecured lines of credit, MSR lines of credit and early buy out facilities, remains unchanged at $31.30 billion. This figure compares with $29.40 billion and $19.13 billion as of December 31, 2020 and December 31, 2019, respectively.
