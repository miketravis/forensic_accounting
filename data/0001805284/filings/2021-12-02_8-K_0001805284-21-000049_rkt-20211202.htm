Item 1.01 Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement.

On December 2, 2021, Rocket Mortgage, LLC (the "Company"), a Michigan limited liability company and indirect subsidiary of Rocket Companies, Inc., as Seller, and UBS AG, by and through its branch office at 1285 Avenue of the Americas, New York, New York, as buyer, entered into Amendment No. 14 to the Amended and Restated Master Repurchase Agreement and Amendment No. 29 to the Pricing Side Letter for the Amended and Restated Master Repurchase Agreement (the "Amendments"), which extended the termination date of the existing Amended and Restated Master Repurchase Agreement dated as of April 10, 2015, as amended, by and between UBS AG, by and through its branch office at 1285 Avenue of the Americas, New York, New York, as buyer, and the Company (the "Master Repurchase Agreement") from December 2, 2021 to December 1, 2022 and effectuated certain other technical changes to the Master Repurchase Agreement.

The foregoing description of the Master Repurchase Agreement, as amended, does not purport to be complete and is subject to, and qualified in its entirety by reference to the full text of the Amendment No. 14 to the Master Repurchase Agreement, a copy of which will be filed with the annual report on Form 10-K of Rocket Companies, Inc.

Following the execution of the Amendments, as of December 2, 2021, the total funding capacity of the Company, including pursuant to all master repurchase agreements, early funding facilities, unsecured lines of credit, MSR lines of credit and early buy out facilities, remains unchanged at $33.9 billion. This figure compares with $29.30 billion and $19.13 billion as of December 31, 2020 and December 31, 2019, respectively.
