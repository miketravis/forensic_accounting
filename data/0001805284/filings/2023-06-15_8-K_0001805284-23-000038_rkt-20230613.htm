Item 5.07    Submission of Matters to a Vote of Security Holders.

The 2023 Annual Meeting of Stockholders (the “Annual Meeting”) of Rocket Companies, Inc. (the “Company”) was held on June 13, 2023.

At the Annual Meeting, the Company’s stockholders:

(1)    Elected the two Class III director nominees, each director to hold office until the 2026 annual meeting of stockholders and until such director’s successor is duly elected and qualified, subject to earlier resignation, retirement or other termination of service;

(2)    Ratified the appointment of Ernst & Young LLP as the Company’s independent registered public accounting firm for the year ending December 31, 2023; and

(3)    Approved an amendment to the Amended and Restated Rocket Companies, Inc. 2020 Team Member Stock Purchase Plan.

The Annual Meeting proposals and voting requirements are described in more detail in the Company’s definitive proxy statement filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission on April 26, 2023.
