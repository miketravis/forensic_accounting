Item 1.01. Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement.

On June 30, 2021, loanDepot.com, LLC, a Delaware limited liability company and an indirect, majority-owned subsidiary of loanDepot, Inc. (the “Company”), as seller, entered into Amendment No. 15 (“Amendment 15”) to the Master Repurchase Agreement, dated as of June 3, 2016 (as amended, restated, supplemented or otherwise modified from time to time, the “MRA”) with JPMorgan Chase Bank, N.A., as buyer (“JPM”) pursuant to which the Company may sell to, and later repurchase from, JPM certain newly originated mortgage loans. The primary purposes of Amendment 15 are to (a) amend the Margin Call mechanics and (b) amend the uncommitted amount available under the MRA to be $1,000,000,000 so that the aggregate facility amount continues to be $1,100,000,000 via certain ancillary agreements thereto.
