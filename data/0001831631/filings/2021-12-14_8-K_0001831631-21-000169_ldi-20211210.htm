Item 1.01. Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement.

On December 10, 2021, loanDepot.com, LLC, a Delaware limited liability company and an indirect, majority-owned subsidiary of loanDepot, Inc. (the “Company”), as seller, entered into Amendment No. 17 (“Amendment 17”) to the Master Repurchase Agreement, dated as of June 3, 2016 (as amended, restated, supplemented or otherwise modified from time to time, the “MRA”) with JPMorgan Chase Bank, N.A., as buyer (“JPM”) pursuant to which the Company may sell to, and later repurchase from, JPM certain newly originated mortgage loans. The primary purposes of Amendment 17 are to (a) extend the Termination Date until October 10, 2022, and amend the definitions of Affiliate, Change in Control, and Ineligible Mortgage Loan and (b) through ancillary agreements, amend the definitions of Eligible Mortgage Loan and Purchase Price.

The foregoing description of Amendment 17 does not purport to be complete and is qualified in its entirety by reference to the full text of Amendment 17, a copy of which is attached hereto as Exhibit 10.1 and is incorporated herein by reference.
