1.01 Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement.

Amendment No. 9 to Master Repurchase Agreement with Credit Suisse First Boston Mortgage Capital LLC

On February 16, 2022, loanDepot.com, LLC, a Delaware limited liability company and an indirect subsidiary of loanDepot, Inc. (the “Company”), as seller, entered into Amendment No. 9 (“Amendment 9”) to the Master Repurchase Agreement, dated as of March 10, 2017 (as amended, restated, supplemented or otherwise modified from time to time, the “MRA”) with Credit Suisse First Boston Mortgage Capital LLC, as administrative agent, Credit Suisse AG, a company incorporated in Switzerland, acting through its Cayman Island Branch, as a buyer (“CS AG”), and Alpine Securitization LTD., as a buyer (“Alpine” and together with CS AG, the “Buyers”) pursuant to which the Company may sell to, and later repurchase from, the Buyers certain mortgage loans. The primary purposes of Amendment 9 are to (a) change definitions related to the Benchmark, including replacing LIBOR with SOFR for interest rate calculations and (b) amend certain pricing provisions via certain ancillary agreements thereto.

The foregoing description of Amendment 9 does not purport to be complete and is subject to, and qualified in its entirety, by reference to the full text of the Amendment, which is attached to this report as Exhibit 10.1.
