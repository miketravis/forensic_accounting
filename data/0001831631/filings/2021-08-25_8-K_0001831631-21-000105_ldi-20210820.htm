Item 1.01. Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement.

Second Amended and Restated Master Repurchase Agreement with Bank of America, N.A. and related Guaranty

On August 20, 2021, loanDepot BA Warehouse, LLC, a Delaware limited liability company and a direct, wholly-owned subsidiary loanDepot.com, LLC, as seller (the “Seller), entered into a Second Amended and Restated Master Repurchase Agreement (the “Master Repurchase Agreement”) with loanDepot.com, LLC, a Delaware limited liability company and an indirect, majority-owned subsidiary of loanDepot, Inc., as guarantor and pledgor (the “Company”), and Bank of America, N.A., a national banking association, as buyer (the “Buyer”), pursuant to which the Seller may sell to the Buyer, and later repurchase, participation interests in residential mortgage loans that were issued to the Seller by the Company. The Master Repurchase Agreement and certain ancillary agreements provide for uncommitted financing of $600.00 million. The expiration date of the Master Repurchase Agreement is September 27, 2021, unless extended or earlier terminated in accordance with the terms of the Master Repurchase Agreement. Borrowings under the Master Repurchase Agreement accrue interest at rates per annum calculated as the one-month LIBOR, or successor rate, plus an applicable margin. The Master Repurchase Agreement amends and restates an amended and restated master repurchase agreement, dated as of June 1, 2015, as amended, restated, supplemented or otherwise modified from time to time, entered into by the Company and the Buyer.

The Master Repurchase Agreement contains representations, warranties, covenants, conditions precedent to funding, events of default and indemnities that are customary for agreements of these types. Additionally, the Master Repurchase Agreement provides that the Seller is required to cure any margin deficit at the request of the Buyer.

Should any event of default occur, the financing of mortgage loans under the Master Repurchase Agreement may be terminated and the repurchase of any participation interests sold under the Master Repurchase Agreement could be accelerated to be immediately due and payable at the repurchase price.

Pursuant to a Guaranty, dated as of August 20, 2021 (the “Guaranty Agreement”), from the Company, as guarantor in favor of Buyer, the Company guarantees the payment and performance by the Seller of its obligations under the Master Repurchase Agreement as specified in the Guaranty Agreement.

The foregoing descriptions of the Master Repurchase Agreement and the Guaranty Agreement are not complete and are qualified in their entirety by reference to the full text of the Master Repurchase Agreement and the Guaranty Agreement, copies of which are attached hereto as Exhibits 10.1 and 10.2, respectively, and are incorporated herein by reference.

Amendment No. 16 to Amended and Restated Mortgage Loan Participation Purchase and Sale Agreement with Bank of America, N.A.

On August 20, 2021, the Company entered into Amendment No. 16 (“Amendment 16”) to the Amended and Restated Mortgage Loan Purchase and Sale Agreement, dated as of July 17, 2015, with Bank of America, N.A., a national banking association, as purchaser (the “Purchaser”) and the Company as seller, pursuant to which the Company may sell to, and later repurchase from, the Purchaser the Company’s beneficial right, title and interest in and to designated pools of residential mortgage loans eligible in the aggregate to back securities. The primary purpose of Amendment 16 is to amend the definition of “Master Repurchase Agreement” to refer to the Master Repurchase Agreement described above.

The foregoing description of Amendment 16 is not complete and is qualified in its entirety by reference to the full text of the Amendment 16, a copy of which is attached hereto as Exhibit 10.3 and is incorporated herein by reference.
