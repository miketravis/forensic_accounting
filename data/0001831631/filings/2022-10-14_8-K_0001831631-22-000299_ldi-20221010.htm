Item 1.01    Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement.


On October 10, 2022, loanDepot.com, LLC, a Delaware limited liability company and an indirect subsidiary of loanDepot, Inc. (the “Company”), as seller, entered into Amendment No. 10 (“Amendment No. 10”) to the Mortgage Loan Participation Sale Agreement, dated as of August 15, 2016 (as amended, restated, supplemented, or otherwise modified from time to time, the “MLPSA”) with JP Morgan Chase Bank, National Association (“JP Morgan”), as purchaser, pursuant to which the Company may sell to JP Morgan participation interests in certain designated pools of fully amortizing first lien residential mortgage loans eligible to back securities and, at a later date, cause such mortgage loans to back a security issued by the Company, which will be delivered to JP Morgan in exchange for the related participation certificate. Along with certain technical changes, the primary purposes of Amendment No. 10 is to extend the termination date to November 11, 2022.

The foregoing description of Amendment No. 10 is not complete and is qualified in its entirety by reference to the full text of Amendment No. 10, a copy of which is attached hereto as Exhibit 10.1 and is incorporated herein by reference.
