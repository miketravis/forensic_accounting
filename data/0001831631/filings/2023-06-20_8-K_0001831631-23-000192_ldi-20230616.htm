Item 1.02     Termination of a Material Definitive Agreement.

loanDepot.com, LLC, a Delaware limited liability company and an indirect subsidiary of loanDepot, Inc. (the “Company”), and NexBank, as lender, were parties to that certain Credit and Security Agreement, dated as of October 29, 2014 (as amended, restated, supplemented or otherwise modified from time to time, the “Credit Agreement”) pursuant to which NexBank, as lender, extended a revolving line of credit available to the Company that was primarily secured by certain of the Company’s mortgage servicing rights under the Company’s servicing agreement with Freddie Mac. On June 16, 2023, the Credit Agreement reached it maturity date and loanDepot and NexBank mutually decided to allow the credit facility to terminate in accordance with its terms.
